Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Going Once!

What kind of game is Going Once!?

▼

A cozy art-inspection and auction house simulator where you examine paintings, negotiate with NPCs, manage your gallery, and run intense live auctions.

What platforms will it be available on?

▼

PC (Steam).

Does the game have a story?

Who is the target audience?

▼

Casual gamers around the age of 10 to 21 looking for a cozy management simulator with elements of Papers, Please. The game features buying art from NPCs, managing a gallery, and auctioning off art.

Is there character customization?

▼

Not currently, but it is in development for later releases.

What is the age rating for the game?

▼

E for Everyone (10+).

What languages are supported?

▼

Only English currently, but we plan to make it more language inclusive in further development.

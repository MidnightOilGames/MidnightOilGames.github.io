Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Going Once!

What kind of game is Going Once!?

A cozy art-inspection and auction house simulator where you examine paintings, negotiate with NPCs, examine your gallery, and run intense live auctions.

What platforms will it be available on?

PC (Steam).

Does the game have a story?

Who is our target audience?

Our target audience are casual gamers around the age of 10 to 21 looking for a cozy management simulator game with elements of Papers, Please. The game will feature buying art from NPCs, managing a gallery of art and auctioning off art.

Is there character customization?

Not currently, but it is in development for later releases.

What is the age rating for the game?

E for everyone (10+)

What languages are supported?

Only English currently, but we plan to make it more language inclusive in further development.

Inspect. Curate. Auction.

Running an art auction house has never been more intense—or cozy.

🔍 Inspect Fine Art

Use tools to discover tags on each art piece and uncover its true value. Haggle with sellers for a lower price.

🏛️ Arrange Your Gallery

Arrange pieces in your showroom, learn NPC preferences through dialogue, and prepare your gallery for auction night.

🔨 Run Live Auctions

Watch NPCs auto-bid on your art while you handle quick time events to keep the auction going.

PHASE I — BUYING

Backroom deals and risky purchases.

Meet sellers, inspect their art using the tools, and decide whether to buy, haggle, or reject.

PHASE II — GALLERY

Manage the floor and observe your guests.

Arrange paintings and sculptures on display, talk to NPCs, and learn their tastes. The relationships you build here affect how aggressively they bid during auctions.

PHASE III — AUCTION

Under the spotlight, every choice is public.

NPCs auto-bid on your art pieces while you react to quick time events that affect the final sale price. Keep up with the pace or watch your profits slip.
